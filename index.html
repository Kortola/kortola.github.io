About the Artist

I am a painter working in acrylics on large scale canvases and intimate wood panels. Born in Brazil into a family with deep roots in Valencia, Spain, my work is a dialogue between the vibrant colors of South America and the Mediterranean tradition of light. My technique is grounded in this dual heritage, using direct brushwork and a natural sense of color to interpret the beauty of everyday scenes.

I studied fine arts at the Escola Panamericana de Arte in São Paulo, where the city itself was my first subject: urban, colorful, and chaotic. It was there that I discovered the energy of color that still defines my palette today.

Moving to Indiana changed everything. Surrounded by open skies, still water, and the unhurried rhythm of the Midwest, I discovered what I had been looking for all along: quiet. The chaos gave way to calm, the urban density to open horizon, and the noise to light.

Today, I paint the landscapes that surround me and the quiet moments found in still life. I always begin with light with the same intention: to paint something that makes people stop, breathe, and feel at peace. People who see my paintings often tell me they feel calm just looking at them. That is the highest thing I could hope to achieve.

Based in Northwest Indiana and conveniently located near Chicago, I welcome local collectors and am happy to arrange personal delivery or ship artwork nationwide.
